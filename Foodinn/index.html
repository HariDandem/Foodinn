Frequently Asked Questions

Is Foodies’ Bread Really Baked Fresh Each Day?

Yes, all of our bread is freshly baked every morning using high-quality ingredients. We believe in delivering freshness and flavor in every loaf.

Do You Use Animal Fats or By-products in Your Baked Goods?

No, our breads are 100% vegetarian and free from animal fats or by-products. We’re committed to offering clean and ethical food choices.

Are Your Products Suitable for Vegans?

Most of our breads are vegan-friendly, but we recommend checking individual product labels or asking our staff for specific details.

Do You Offer Gluten-Free Options?

Yes, we offer a selection of gluten-free breads for customers with dietary restrictions. Availability may vary daily.
